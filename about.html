Made in Karachi

Bags for people who don't sit still.

Bags Hub started with one idea: everyday carry shouldn't mean choosing between quality and price. Every bag is sourced, tested and sold by people who actually use them — commuting, studying, hustling.

🧵

How it started

From a counter in Gulshan-e-Iqbal

Bags Hub didn't start in a showroom. It started with a small counter in Karachi's mobile accessories market, watching what people actually carry every day — and noticing the gap between cheap bags that fall apart and imported ones priced out of reach.

What we believe

Built for how you move, not how a catalog looks

Every bag we sell is chosen or made because it solves a real problem — a laptop that needs protecting, a commute that needs a free hand, a weekend that needs one bag instead of three.

🚶

What We Care About

💰

Fair pricing

No middlemen markup. We price bags for what they cost to make, not what a brand name lets us charge.

🧭

Real testing

Every bag is used before it's sold — zippers, straps, stitching, all tested on actual Karachi streets.

📦

COD, always

Pay when it arrives. No advance required, no surprises.
